At Nightfall, the Goldfish

This is an interactive story about life and metempsychosis.
Each chapter in the "map" tells one character's life experience, using the first person.
Characters' stories are linked closely to each other. Similar but different.
This piece uses many symbols of life and death. To some extent, these stories represent different periods of life. They indicate the whole life of the young boy, from a child to an old man.
Readers have full freedom to read in their preferred order to experience and to interpret.


Read the whole story without interaction here if you like.
